The Proposal: -
Planning Department (ROHYO) Ward No. Under Section 1, the State has approved a scheme to finance the repair work of irrigation wells to help farmers in respect of irrigation wells damaged due to heavy rainfall / flood situation during June to September, 2025 in the state.
Under the scheme, out of 22,135 dilapidated wells, 16,787 administrative sanctioned wells have so far been sanctioned as advance under the head "Preparation of wells under drought mitigation measures (2505A049) 39, Subsidiary Grants (Post Pay)" amounting to Rs. 25.18 crores has been disbursed. The program was intended to run for an additional year 2025-26.
However, due to excessive rainfall / flood situation from June to September 2025 as well as stagnant water in the well, non-availability of labour, etc., siltation in all the administrative approved wells has not been fully removed. The repair work has not been completed yet. The background of Elnino in the state is Ma. In view of the overall suggestion made by the Chief Minister in this regard, the Government was considering an extension to complete the incomplete repair works of the wells under the scheme. Accordingly, the following decisions are being taken.
Government Decision: - "The date is being extended till 39th December, 2026 for completion of incomplete works for repair of irrigation wells damaged and eroded due to heavy rainfall / flood situation in the State during the year 2025-26 from June to September, 2025." "The Terms, Conditions and Conditions in the Government Decision dated 13.10.2025 to this Government Decision shall remain in force.
